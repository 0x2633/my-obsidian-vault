## Course 1 - Foundation of Project Management

### What is project management

- A series of tasks that need to be completed to reach desired outcome.
- Usually includes a seat for unique deliverables
- A temporary pursuit; it has a defined beginning and end
- It has **clear outcome** **and start and end date**
- Poor project management might cause 48% of project miss delivery dates and 43% of project miss budget targets
### Poor project management

It might cause:
- 48% of project miss delivery dates
- 43% of project miss budget target
- 31% of project fail to meet an organization's goals

### Daily life of a PM
- Plaining and organization
- Managing tasks
- Budgeting and controlling costs and other factors
